Solidity Contract

Below is a solidity contract that I wrote in order to gain familiarity with the solidity documentation and syntax. The contract is simple and allows members to withdraw less than 1 ether no more than 3 times. Members can only be added by the creator of the contract. In the future our club may use this to let students interact with wallets and contracts for free, likely on a test network.
